Activity Overview

This Webcast contains video and downloadable slides from the meeting Striking Success: The Emergence of New Targeted Treatment Strategies for Advanced Breast Cancer, a prIME Oncology closed activity that was held on December 12, 2014, in San Antonio, Texas.

The information in this activity is intended for healthcare professionals based outside of the United States.

This activity may contain information on products outside the approved indications where you practice.
