Activity Overview

This CME-certified Webcast contains video and downloadable slides from the symposium Delivering Chemotherapy With Confidence: New Strategies to Minimize Treatment-Induced Nausea and Vomiting, a prIME Oncology educational activity that was held at the 2014 ESMO Congress in Madrid, Spain, on 26 September 2014.
