Target Audiance

This educational activity is specifically designed to meet the needs of medical, surgical, and radiation oncologists, neurologists, neuro-oncologists, and other healthcare professionals involved in the treatment of patients with cancer.

Learning Objectives

After successful completion of this educational activity, participants should be able to:

  • Integrate recent findings for managing lymphomatous meningitis into clinical practice, including promising emerging agents
  • Identify appropriate techniques for diagnosing and assessing neoplastic meningitis
  • Evaluate emerging options for the treatment of carcinomatous meningitis due to HER2-positive breast cancer
