Activity Overview

This CME-certified Webcast contains video and downloadable slides from the symposium Transformed Care for Advanced Medullary Thyroid Cancer: Incorporating the Right Treatments for the Right Patient at the Right Time, a prIME Oncology educational activity that was held on 7 September 2014 in Santiago de Compostela, Spain.
