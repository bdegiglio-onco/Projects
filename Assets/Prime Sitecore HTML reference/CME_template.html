Learning Objectives:
After successful completion of this educational activity, participants should be able to:

  • Identify best practices for implementing therapeutic drug monitoring and managing hypersensitivity to optimize the use of asparaginase therapy in patients with acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL)
  • Employ recent advances regarding the utilization of asparaginase therapy in patients with ALL based on emerging clinical trial data and current treatment recommendations
