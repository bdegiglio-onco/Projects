Target Audience

This educational activity is intended for practicing hematologists, oncologists, and other allied healthcare providers who manage and/or treat patients with T-cell lymphoma.


Learning Objectives

After successful completion of this educational activity, participants should be able to:

  • Evaluate recent advances in the understanding of T-cell lymphoma tumor biology and the development of effective novel therapies
  • Identify optimal treatment strategies for patients with T-cell lymphoma based on current treatment guidelines
  • Develop strategies to integrate the use of novel agents for patients with newly-diagnosed and relapsed T-cell lymphoma.
