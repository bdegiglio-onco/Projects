Target Audience

This educational activity is designed for medical oncologists, surgical and radiation oncologists, and other healthcare professionals involved and/or interested in the management of patients with breast cancer.


Learning Objectives

After successful completion of this educational activity, participants should be able to:

  • Summarize the current status of neoadjuvant therapy for breast cancer and important unanswered questions in this setting pertaining to therapy selection and administration
  • Describe ongoing efforts to individualize neoadjuvant therapy for specific breast cancer subtypes and patient populations
  • Discuss emerging clinical trial data and ongoing studies investigating optimal chemotherapy selection and dosing schedules in the neoadjuvant setting
  • Evaluate supportive care measures for patients treated with dose-dense neoadjuvant regimens
