Learning Objectives
After successful completion of this educational activity, participants should be able to:

  • Evaluate recent findings from studies investigating biomarkers and molecular features of hematologic malignancies and disorders
  • Apply emerging data from clinical  trials evaluating novel agents and treatment approaches for hematologic malignancies and disorders
  • Identify appropriate clinical trials and refer eligible patients with hematologic malignancies and disorders for enrollment to expand their treatment options
