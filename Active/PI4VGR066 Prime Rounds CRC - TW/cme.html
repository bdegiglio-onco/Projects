Learning Objectives
After completion of this educational activity, participants should be able to:

  • Evaluate emerging clinical trial data in metastatic colorectal cancer (mCRC), including studies focused on the safety and efficacy of novel targeted therapies
  • Identify strategies to individualize treatment selection and sequencing for patients with mCRC based on mutation testing, extent of metastatic disease, and other patient and disease characteristics
  • Describe best practices for monitoring and managing treatment-associated adverse events in patients with mCRC to improve patient care
