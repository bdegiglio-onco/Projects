Target Audience

This educational activity is specifically designed for healthcare providers interested and/or involved in the treatment of patients with solid tumors.

» View Activity

Learning Objectives

  • Describe the different types of NGS and the information provided by the currently available analytical platforms
  • Identify tumor types for which NGS may be an appropriate strategy to assist with treatment decision making
  • Explain the relevance of detecting actionable mutations in solid tumors and their potential importance for treatment selection
