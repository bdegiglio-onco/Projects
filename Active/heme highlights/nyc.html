Target Audience

This educational activity is intended for hematologists, oncologists, oncology specialist nurses, and other allied healthcare professionals interested in the management of hematologic malignancies.

Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this educational activity, participants should be better able to:

  • Evaluate recent data from studies examining the role of biomarkers in hematologic malignancies or hematologic disorders
  • Integrate new data from trials evaluating current treatment options for hematologic malignancies or hematologic disorders
  • Employ recent data from trials investigating novel therapies and approaches for treatment of hematologic malignancies or hematologic disorders
  • Identify appropriate clinical trials for eligible patients with hematologic malignancies or hematologic disorders in order to expand their treatment options
