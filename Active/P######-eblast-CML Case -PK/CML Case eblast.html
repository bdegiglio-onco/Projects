InterActive Clinical Scenario: Late Milestones and Treatment Discontinuation in Patients with CML

This CME-certified case presentation contains interactive video and case voting with commentary customized to each case option, in addition to downloadable slides to complement the interactive case. This educational activity provides physicians with guidance on monitoring patients with chronic myeloid leukemia (CML), late milestones, and treatment discontinuation.
