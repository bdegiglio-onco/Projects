Topic Description

The Year in Review will provide participants with an overview of the major developments in the field of immuno-oncology in 2014. This will include a review of emerging immunotherapies for melanoma and lung cancer, as well as a look at what new developments can be expected in 2015. This interactive meeting will feature a combination of lectures, quizzes, and lively discussion to challenge and inform attendees.


Target Audience

This educational activity is specifically designed for medical oncologists, nurses, and other healthcare professionals involved in the treatment of patients with cancer in the United States.


Learning Objectives

After successful completion of this educational activity, participants should be able to:

  • Explain the rationale for developing immunotherapies for the treatment of cancer
  • Discuss emerging data from clinical trials of immunotherapies for melanoma and lung cancer
  • Identify ongoing clinical trials of immunotherapies in which to enroll patients when appropriate
